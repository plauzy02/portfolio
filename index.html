Mock Project — Kinetic was created as a speculative application task for Heidi Health's Junior Product Program. It is not a live product. All data, clinic names, and figures are fictional and for demonstration only.
The Problem

Most clinics want to receive shared patient records but won't share their own. Kinetic's thesis: adoption only works when sharing is the rational self-interested choice, not a prosocial one.

Design Philosophy
•Simplified the UI especially in high-traffic areas like dashboards — when interacting constantly you don't want a jumble of words.
•Added quick-view icons on dashboards & key areas to allow users to glimpse key info without committing much.
•Colour scheme of sage green & muted grey — sage green is inviting & reflects the friendly service. Muted grey highlights interactive elements without adding too much colour.
•Little cartoon dogs scattered throughout to emphasise the system's friendly & carefree vibe. Making key information very visible was key.

Sigma Dog Care
COMP2750 — University Project · Figma Prototype

A mobile UI/UX system designed for COMP2750 at Macquarie University. Sigma Dog Care connects dog owners with local carers for short-term daycare. The prototype covers both the owner flow (create ad, manage applicants, confirm a carer) and the carer flow (search ads, apply, manage profile & income) — plus full error state coverage. All screens, interactions, and user flows were designed in Figma.
